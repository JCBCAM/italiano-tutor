Lezione 1

Primi contatti

Your first words of Italian — how to greet people, say who you are, where you’re from, and count to twenty. Listen, build, and say each one out loud.

By the end you’ll be able to

  • ✓ Greet someone — morning, evening, hello & goodbye
  • ✓ Ask and say your name
  • ✓ Say where you’re from and your nationality
  • ✓ Use the verb essere (to be)
  • ✓ Count from zero to twenty
